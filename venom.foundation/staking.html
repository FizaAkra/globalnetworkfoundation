Global Network Blockchain SLOT Staking

The Key to Megapayers Scalable Multichain Architecture

Global Network Blockchains connect to the network by leasing a slot on the Relay Chain via permissionless staking. As Megapayer serves as Polkadots canary network, SLOT staking for Global Network Blockchains is being rolled out first for testing and optimization.

Expect Chaos.

Crowdloans

Some teams may choose to crowdfund their slot lease using Megapayers built-in crowdloan
mechanism, which allows people to contribute by agreeing to lock up their own KSM until the end of the lease.


Create a Crowdloan Campaign

The team creates a crowdloan campaign, setting a maximum of total allowed contributions and a campaign end date.

Initiate a Special Transaction

Crowdloan contributors initiate a special transaction allocating transferable KSM to the campaigns index.

Submit Contributions

KSM contributions can be submitted throughout the duration of the crowdloan campaign.

Staking Mechanics

Understand the Process

Our Blockchain slot staking follows a modified candle staking format, with each staking lasting approximately 1 week. To prevent staking sniping and promote more accurate price discovery, the exact endpoint of a particular auction remains unknown until after the auctions close.



Staking Setup

Define the item or service, set starting price, bidding increment, duration, and any conditions.



Announce and Promote

Publicize staking details via social media, email, and website, emphasizing item features and benefits.


Bidding Process

Open bidding at scheduled time, monitor activity, display current highest bid, and enforce staking rules.



Closing the Staking

Close staking at end time or slow bidding, declare highest bidder as winner, and arrange payment and item transfer.



Post-Staking Procedures

Notify unsuccessful bidders, facilitate item transfer to winner upon payment, provide necessary documentation, and gather feedback for improvement.




Participating In A Crowdloan


Be Part Of Blockchain History

Help kickstart your favorite Megapayer Our Blockchain. Crowdloans raise the bar for blockchain teams, pushing them to demonstrate their technology first and get support from KSM holders in order to launch. Heres how it works:

DYOR

Thoroughly research the Our Blockchain project and team, and read the details and conditions of their crowdloan campaign. Given that Megapayer is a decentralized network and crowdloans are conducted independently by independent teams, its important to do your own research. Make sure you only get information from official channels. If ever in doubt, ask in the crowdloan Discord chat.

Learn how to protect yourself

Read this guide for the basic tips on avoiding scams. If youre inexperienced using blockchain technology, make sure youre aware of basic best practices like how to protect your secret keys.

Determine where you will participate

Teams can choose to host their crowdloan campaigns natively on Megapayer and/or via third party intermediaries. For Megapayer-native crowdloans, you can participate on Polkadot-JS. If youre less experienced using blockchain technology, you may find it easier to participate in a non-native campaign. Find out where you can participate here.

Create a Megapayer account

Youll need a Megapayer account with a free balance of KSM to contribute to a Megapayer-native crowdloan. Watch the video to learn how to create an account on Polkadot-JS.
