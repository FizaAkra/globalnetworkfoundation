Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Global Network Foundation?

The Global Network Foundation is an organization dedicated to creating an EVM-based blockchain project focused on low-cost transfers, scalability, transparency, and reliability. Our mission is to develop a secure and innovative blockchain ecosystem for various applications, including privacy-focused social media and messaging on the Web 3 network.

What is the Megapayer wallet?

The Megapayer wallet is a secure and user-friendly digital wallet developed by the Global Network Foundation. It allows users to store, buy, and sell cryptocurrencies through a peer-to-peer platform. The wallet features multi-factor authentication and encryption standards to ensure the safety of users' assets.

What is the stablecoin offered by Megapayer wallet?

The Megapayer wallet includes its own stablecoin, designed to provide a reliable digital currency for exchanging crypto assets. This stablecoin helps maintain value stability, making it easier for users to transact without worrying about market volatility.

How does Global Network ensure security?

We use multi-factor authentication, encryption, and security audits, and run bug bounty programs to maintain high security.

What are the steps for launching the mainnet?

Steps include testing on the testnet, conducting security audits, and planning a smooth transition to the mainnet.

How can I participate in the token sale and airdrop?

Follow our official announcements for instructions on how to join the token sale and airdrop, ensuring compliance with all guidelines.
